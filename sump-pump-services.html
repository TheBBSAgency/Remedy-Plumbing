Sump Pump Repair & Replacement in Fond du Lac, WI

Sump pump repair and replacement before Wisconsin's snowmelt turns your basement into a problem.

Don't Wait Until Your Basement Is Wet

A failed sump pump is a Wisconsin homeowner's nightmare — usually discovered in spring when the ground is saturated and the water has nowhere to go. The Remedy Plumbing team handles sump pump repairs and replacements throughout Fond du Lac and surrounding communities.

Wisconsin's freeze-thaw cycles create specific issues: corrugated discharge hoses freeze solid, preventing the pump from draining; float switches stick after a long winter; and pumps that sat dormant all winter sometimes don't come back. We see it every spring.

Pro tip: Remove your black corrugated discharge hose from the pump before winter sets in. If that hose freezes solid, your pump has nowhere to push water — it will run continuously and burn out. Disconnect it in fall, reconnect in spring.

Common Problems We Fix

  • Burned-out pump motor from a frozen discharge line
  • Stuck or failed float switch not triggering the pump
  • Frozen discharge lines blocking water flow
  • Pump running but not removing water
  • Complete pump failure after extended dormancy
  • Aging systems past their useful life
