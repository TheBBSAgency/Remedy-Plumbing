Why Wisconsin Plumbing Is Different

Hard Water. Hard Winters. Your Plumber Needs to Know Both.

Eastern Wisconsin's water is hard — loaded with minerals that shorten water heater lifespans and clog small fixtures. Add below-zero winters and homes that range from 1880s to brand-new, and you've got a unique plumbing environment. The Remedy Plumbing team has worked in these conditions for over 20 years.
