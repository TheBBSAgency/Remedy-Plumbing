Water Heater Replacement in Fond du Lac, WI

Gas, electric, and hybrid water heater installation. Most jobs completed within 24 hours. Upfront pricing before we start.

Fond du Lac's Water Heater Experts

Water heater replacement is the most common call we get — and for good reason. Water heaters in Wisconsin age faster than the national average because of the region's hard water. Mineral buildup attacks the tank lining and heating elements, and most units in this area are worn out well before their warranty expires.

Remedy Plumbing installs gas, electric, and hybrid (heat pump) water heaters for homes and businesses across Fond du Lac and eastern Wisconsin. We stock common sizes and can typically complete your installation the same day or the next.

Types We Install

  • Gas water heaters — standard atmospheric vent and power vent models
  • Electric water heaters — straightforward replacement when gas isn't an option
  • Hybrid / heat pump water heaters — 4x more efficient than standard electric; great for homes with adequate space and airflow

We're honest about what makes sense for your home. If you have gas, we'll generally recommend staying with gas. If you're considering a tankless unit, we'll walk you through the real math on whether it pays off in this water hardness — most of the time, it doesn't.

Signs Your Water Heater Needs Replacing

  • Water around the base of the unit
  • Rust-colored hot water
  • Unit is 7+ years old (especially in hard-water areas)
  • Inconsistent or lukewarm hot water
  • Rumbling or popping sounds from sediment buildup
  • Unit looks visibly corroded or worn
💡 Pro Tip — Extend Your Water Heater's Life: Your water heater has an anode rod inside that draws mineral impurities away from the tank. When that rod deteriorates, the tank itself starts corroding. Replacing the anode rod every 2–3 years can significantly extend your unit's lifespan. You can pick one up at Menards for around $40.
