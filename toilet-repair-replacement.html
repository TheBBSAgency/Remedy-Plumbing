Toilet Repair & Replacement in Fond du Lac, WI

Running toilets, leaks, weak flushing, or full replacements. Flat-rate pricing, in and out clean.

Fast Toilet Fixes with Upfront Pricing

A running toilet wastes hundreds of gallons of water a week. A leaking base is a slow disaster waiting to happen. The Remedy Plumbing team handles toilet repairs and full replacements for homes and businesses throughout eastern Wisconsin.

Full toilet replacement is one of our flat-rate services — you know the price before we start. We bring the new fixture, remove the old one, install it properly, test it, and clean up. Done.

Common Problems We Fix

  • Running toilet — worn flapper, fill valve, or flush valve
  • Leaking at the base — failed wax ring
  • Weak or incomplete flushing — mineral buildup or worn internal parts
  • Unstable or rocking toilet
  • Cracked tank or bowl
  • Old toilet ready for full replacement
  • Toilet that clogs frequently
