Who We Are

Built on Honest Work & a Good Attitude

Remedy Plumbing was founded in 2016 by Ian Seth — a Wisconsin Master Plumber who started his apprenticeship in 2003. After years working in the trade, Ian built Remedy Plumbing around a simple idea: show up on time, be straight with people about what needs to be done, and leave the place cleaner than you found it.

Today the Remedy Plumbing team brings that same approach to every job — from single-family homes in Fond du Lac to commercial accounts across eastern Wisconsin.
