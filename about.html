Our Story

Built Around Honest Service

Remedy Plumbing was founded in 2016 by Ian Seth after nearly 15 years in the trade. Working for another plumbing company, Ian had grown a major property management account nearly on his own — handling scheduling, job management, and customer relationships. When his employer pointed out he was basically running his own business inside theirs, the idea clicked.

He spent a month thinking about the right name. He was always the guy in the shop who could troubleshoot any problem — someone once said, "Ian always has the remedy." That stuck. The Rx logo, the tagline — it all came together from there.

The name also fit the customer side of things. People never call a plumber because things are going great. They call because they need a remedy. Remedy Plumbing exists to provide that — quickly, honestly, and without making people feel like a burden for having a problem.
