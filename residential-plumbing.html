Residential Plumbing Services in Fond du Lac, WI

Full-service residential plumbing for homeowners across eastern Wisconsin. Licensed, honest, and priced fairly.

Your Home Deserves a Plumber Who Treats It Like Their Own

The Remedy Plumbing team handles residential plumbing across Fond du Lac and eastern Wisconsin — from quick fixture swaps to full remodel plumbing. We work with homeowners directly, show up on time, give you a straight price, and clean up when we're done.

Wisconsin homes come with their own set of challenges: hard water, cold winters, older cast iron and galvanized pipes, and houses that range from century-old to brand new. Our team has worked in all of it. We know what to look for and how to fix it right.

Common Problems We Fix

  • Low water pressure — clogged aerators, failing pressure regulators, or supply line issues
  • Leaking pipes — from pinhole leaks to burst lines after a hard freeze
  • Slow drains and blockages in kitchen and bathroom lines
  • Sump pump failures — especially after freeze-thaw cycles in spring
  • Running or leaking toilets wasting water and money
  • Old fixtures and valves that need replacement
  • Water heater failures — especially in homes with hard water
  • Frozen or burst hose bibs from hoses left connected over winter
